ENT

ENT (Otorhinolaryngology) is a specialized branch of medicine that focuses on diagnosing and treating conditions related to the ear, nose, and throat. It covers disorders such as sinusitis, hearing loss, tonsillitis, and voice disorders.

ENT specialists treat conditions such as ear infections, nasal blockages, throat infections, balance disorders, and snoring. They also perform procedures like sinus surgeries, cochlear implants, and tonsillectomies.

Early diagnosis and proper ENT care are crucial for maintaining overall ear, nose, and throat health. Regular check-ups and timely intervention can significantly improve patient outcomes.

Our ENT team at Raksha Hospital comprises highly skilled specialists experienced in treating a wide range of ear, nose, and throat conditions. At Raksha, we provide expert care in:

  • Sinus & Allergy Treatment
  • Hearing Loss & Ear Infections
  • Voice & Speech Disorders
  • Snoring & Sleep Apnea Management
  • Thyroid & Salivary Gland Disorders
  • Pediatric ENT Care

Comprehensive ENT Treatments

Our ENT department offers personalized treatment plans that include:

  • ✔ Medications for infections and allergy relief
  • ✔ Minimally invasive procedures for sinus and throat conditions
  • ✔ Hearing aids & cochlear implants for auditory support
  • ✔ Speech therapy for voice and communication improvement
  • ✔ Advanced diagnostic imaging for ENT assessments
  • ✔ Comprehensive hearing screenings for early detection

ENT conditions can significantly impact daily life and communication. At Raksha Hospital, we focus on early detection, advanced treatments, and preventive ENT care to help patients maintain optimal health.

Symptoms
  • Hearing loss & ear pain
  • Frequent sinus infections
  • Difficulty swallowing
  • Voice changes & hoarseness
  • Nasal congestion & snoring
  • Dizziness & balance issues
Causes
  • Infections & allergies
  • Exposure to loud noise
  • Sinus congestion & polyps
  • Throat inflammation
  • Genetic predisposition
  • Environmental pollutants
Treatment
  • Medications & antibiotics
  • Hearing aids & ear tubes
  • Sinus surgery & nasal treatments
  • Speech therapy & voice training
  • Tonsillectomy & adenoidectomy
  • Balance disorder management
Care
  • Regular ENT check-ups
  • Protecting ears from loud noise
  • Using a humidifier for nasal care
  • Maintaining vocal hygiene
  • Avoiding allergens & irritants
  • Practicing good ear & throat hygiene
