We Maintain Cleanliness Rules Inside Our Hospital

We strictly follow cleanliness rules to ensure a safe and hygienic environment for everyone. Maintaining cleanliness is our priority for the well-being of our patients and staff.

Hand Hygiene: Staff must wash hands before and after patient contact.
Disinfection & Sterilization: Equipment and patient areas are sanitized regularly.
Waste Management: Proper disposal of biomedical waste prevents contamination.
Air & Water Purification: Ensuring clean air and water in hospital environments.
Use of PPE: Staff wear gloves, masks, and gowns to prevent infections.

We Offer Different Services To Improve Your Health

We provide a range of specialized services to enhance your health and well-being.

Paediatric Surgery

Pediatric surgery involves the surgical care of infants, children, and adolescents, addressing congenital conditions, injuries, and diseases in young patients.

Neuro Surgery & Spine

Neurosurgery and spine care focus on treating brain, spinal cord, and nerve disorders through advanced surgical techniques and technologies.

Heart Surgery

Treats blocked arteries, valve defects, and heart failure using advanced procedures like bypass surgery and valve replacement to restore heart function.

Ear Treatment

Manages infections, hearing loss, and balance disorders with medication, surgery, or hearing aids to improve ear health and hearing ability.

Vision Problems

Corrects cataracts, glaucoma, and refractive errors through lenses, medication, or surgeries like LASIK to restore clear vision.

Blood Transfusion

Replenishes lost blood due to surgery, injury, or conditions like anemia and leukemia, helping restore oxygen levels and support recovery.
